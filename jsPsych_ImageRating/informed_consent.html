Welcome to the study

In this task, you will be presented with two similar looking images created by a generative adversarial network (GAN). In each trial, one of the two images is the base image, while the other is the GANâ€™s attempt to create a more/less beautiful version of the base image. In order to validate whether the GAN truly has some understanding of what it means for an image to contain aesthetic value, this study will test whether human participants tend to agree with the GAN on which image is more beautiful. Your task is to indicate which of the two images you believe is more aesthetic. You indicate this with the key on your keyboard corresponding to the location of your preferred image.

Example:

If you prefer image on the left, you press the letter F on your keyboard. If you prefer the image on the right, you press the letter J.

Because this study aims to test the GAN's understanding of aesthetics, it is important that you pick the image that you really feel is more beautiful, and not just what you expect the GAN to produce. There are no right or wrong answers. In some trials the images will be nearly identical, but even here you will have to make a choice. Try not to overthink it, just go with your gut feeling.

The duration of this study is 10 minutes, after which it will automatically end.
